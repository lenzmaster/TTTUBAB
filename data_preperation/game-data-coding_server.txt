Information regarding the coding of the game data, when received from the server

(Number: Meaning)

0: Tile not occupied
1: Tile occupied by player 1
2: Tile occupied by player 2

4: Tile is allowed to be played in by player 1
8: Tile is allowed to be played in by player 2

16: Tile is not occupied, but micro board won by player 1
17: Tile is occupied by player 1 and micro board won by player 1
18: Tile is occupied by player 2, but micro board won by player 1

32: Tile is not occupied, but micro board won by player 2
33: Tile is occupied by player 1, but micro board won by player 2
34: Tile is occupied by player 2 and micro board won by player 2
